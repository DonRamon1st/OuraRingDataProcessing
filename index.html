Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 23, 2025

MyHealthMonitor - OuraRingData collects health data from your Oura Ring via the Oura API V2. This includes Biometric Data (Heart rate, sleep) and Activity Data.

We use your data exclusively to provide visualization services. We do not sell or trade your health data. All data is verified using HMAC SHA256 signatures.
